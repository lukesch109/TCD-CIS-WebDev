Videoball is a minimalist, competitive sports video game developed and published by Action Button Entertainment. It was released on July 12, 2016, for multiple platforms, including PC, PlayStation 4, and Xbox One. The game is known for its simple mechanics, strategic depth, and fast-paced action.

Videoball exemplifies how complex, engaging gameplay can emerge from very simple mechanics. With just a few core actions— movement, shooting, and charging— players can develop deep strategies and tactics. Its design proves that a game doesn't need flashy graphics or complex systems to be fun and engaging.

The development of Videoball highlights the power of simplicity in game design. By focusing on polished mechanics and a clear vision, the team delivered a game that remains an example of elegant, minimalist game development. 

While the specific engine used for Videoball hasn't been publicly disclosed, indie developers often use tools like Unity or GameMaker for similar projects due to their versatility and accessibility.
